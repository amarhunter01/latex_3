\section{Formule}
Zagrade promijenjive veličine:
$$ \Gamma = \left[ \frac{1+\left( \frac{1-x}{1+x}\right)^2 }{x+\frac{3}{4}}\right]^3 $$

Formula u više redova
\begin{align}
4x+y &= 7 \nonumber \\ 
35x-572y &= 125 \nonumber
\end{align}
Formule sa matricama
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
	4 & 1 \\
	35 & -572
\end{bmatrix} 
\begin{bmatrix}
	x \\
	y
\end{bmatrix} 
=
\begin{bmatrix}
	7 \\
	125
\end{bmatrix}
$$
i još jedna
$$
A=
\begin{bmatrix}
	4 & 1 \\
	35 & -572 
\end{bmatrix}
$$
